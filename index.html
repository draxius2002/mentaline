About

MentaLine App

It all began during a historic pandemic in 2020, when World Health Organization (WHO) declared the outbreak of a new coronavirus disease, COVID-19, to be a Public Health Emergency of International Concern. Soon, Stress, Anxiety and Depression echoed throughout the entire world putting millions of people mental wellbeing at risk.

Swift action has to follow. Five university students gathered and decided to grab the baton and work towards helping the ones affected in the community. We embark on a journey participating in various technology start-up challenge as one of the initiatives to resolve the problem at a greater scale.

We promise to ensure users get the best treatment and support anytime, and every time. We work extremely hard to enable users to connect to the best service providers. The fruits of our labour will be the application that one only needs for their mental wellbeing.
